What is your dream?

Dreams are freeform and can be anything.
They could represent a goal or a vision.
They could represent the unattainable or impossible.

What matters is that this dream is yours. You own it.
You can just put it out there, or you could strive to attain it.

It's up to you.

Now describe your dream.

You can be as specific or as vague as you like.
This section is for what your dream looks like when achieved.


Create Dream

Before you can share your dream, you need to assign it categories so that other people can find it!
These can be searched, or simply matched with the interests and skills of others to form their unique Dreamscape.

You can pick as many categories as you like!

If you wish, here you can list the skills you think will be required to achieve your dream
This is useful if you are looking for collaborators, mentors or just friendly advice.

You don't have to add skills.
Some dreams are just dreams and always will be.
